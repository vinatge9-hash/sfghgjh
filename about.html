Our Journey

From a small dream to a community hub

Founded by two friends in Hyderabad, Brew Haven started as a simple pursuit: to make thoughtfully-sourced, well-roasted coffee accessible and inviting. We believe in quality, craft, and the joy of slowing down with a great cup.

We roast locally, support smallholder farmers, and work to ensure fair practices across our supply chain.

Founders

Ananya & Vikram

Passionate about coffee and community, they opened Brew Haven to create a gathering place for craft coffee in Hyderabad.
